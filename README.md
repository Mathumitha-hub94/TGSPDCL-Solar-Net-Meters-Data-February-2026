# ☀️ Solar Net Meter Analysis – TGSPDCL (Feb 2026)

## 📌 Project Overview
*This project focuses on analyzing solar net meter data to understand energy generation, consumption, and efficiency patterns. 
It helps identify how effectively solar energy is utilized and highlights opportunities for optimization.*

### 🎯 Objective
1. Analyze solar energy generation vs consumption
2. Identify consumer usage patterns
3. Detect inefficiencies and irregularities
4. Support data-driven energy optimization decisions

### 📊 Key Concepts
#### 🔁 Net Metering

**Net metering allows consumers to:**
- Use solar energy generated at home
- Export excess energy to the grid
- Import energy when solar production is low

***🛠️ Tools & Technologies***

- **Power BI** – Dashboard & Visualization
- **Python** – Data Cleaning & Analysis

**💡 Key Insights**
1. Many consumers underutilize solar generation
2. Some households are net exporters (earning credits)
3. High dependency on grid indicates inefficient solar usage
4. Peak usage times affect energy import patterns

**🚀 Recommendations**
- Increase awareness of optimal solar usage
- Improve panel efficiency & maintenance
- Encourage energy storage solutions
- Monitor high grid dependency consumers

###### **📌 Conclusion** 
This project provides valuable insights into solar energy efficiency and consumption behavior,
enabling better energy management and sustainability decisions.The solar net meter data shows a positive growth trend,
but adoption is not evenly distributed. With targeted strategies,solar usage can be expanded significantly across all regions.
